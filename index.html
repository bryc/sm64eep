For testing/debugging purposes, the Sound and Language settings are changed by modifying their raw decimal value. Any number between 0 and 65535 is usable. However many values have undefined and unpredictable results.

Known values for Sound setting: Known values for Language setting:
0: Stereo
1: Mono
2: Headset
 0: English
1: French
2: German

Also, a quick note on the level flags:

The first 7 flags represent the stars obtained in that level. The 8th and final flag actually controls to the previous level's cannon state (activated or not).

This accurately reflects the internal data. Blame Nintendo. This will be changeded in a UI update that will make it much more userfriendly.
